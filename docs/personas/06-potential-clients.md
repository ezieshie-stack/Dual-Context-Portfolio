| **Education** | BS Engineering, MBA |
| **Location** | San Francisco, CA |

## Short Bio
Jason runs a Series A startup and is evaluating whether to hire an analytics consultant to build their data foundation. He needs to move fast and prove ROI to investors. Budget is limited but he knows data is critical.

## Goals
- Build analytics MVP quickly
- Prove product-market fit with data
- Impress investors with metrics

## Pain Points
- No data team yet
- Need to move fast on limited budget
- Previous consultants delivered theory, not results

## Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Problem Fit
*I'm evaluating consultants for our analytics needs. I ask "Can you help with growth analytics?" Bot describes relevant experience with churn prediction and customer analytics. Potentially relevant.*

### Scenario 2: Speed Assessment
*Startups move fast. I ask "How quickly can you deliver?" Bot mentions 4-week project timelines. That's startup speed.*

### Scenario 3: Cost Question
*Budget is tight. I ask "What's your day rate?" Bot suggests discussing pricing directly. Fair—I'll reach out.*

### Scenario 4: MVP Scope
*I ask "Can you scope an analytics MVP?" Bot describes phased approach starting with core metrics. Understands startup constraints.*

### Scenario 5: Investor Prep
*I ask "Can you prep for investor due diligence?" Bot shows metrics and business outcome focus. Investor-ready mindset.*

### Scenario 6: KPI Definition
*I ask "Can you help define our KPIs?" Bot describes metric definition methodology. KPI experience.*

### Scenario 7: Dashboard Building
*I ask "Can you build a dashboard?" Bot shows dashboard experience with Invoice Analytics. Can build dashboards.*

### Scenario 8: Ongoing Engagement
*I ask "Do you do ongoing retainer work?" Bot suggests discussing engagement models directly. I'll explore options.*

### Scenario 9: Recommendations
*I ask "What would you recommend we start with?" Bot offers to discuss after understanding context. Consultative approach.*

### Scenario 10: References
*I ask "Can I speak to past clients?" Bot suggests contacting David directly for references. Standard process.*
